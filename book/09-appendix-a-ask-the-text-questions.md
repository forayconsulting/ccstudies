# Appendix A: "Ask the Text" Questions

## What did this text mean to the human author?

- Who were they?
- When did they write this?
- Where did they live?
- What were they addressing?
- What was in *their* Bible?

## What does this text teach me about God?

- What is God doing or saying in this text?
- Why is God doing or saying it?
- How is God doing or saying it?
- Which of God's attributes are on display in this text?

## How should this text contribute to my theology?

- How does this text advance the metanarrative of Scripture?
- How does this text point to Christ?
- What other passages of Scripture clarify and/or illustrate this text?
- What areas of systematic theology relate to this text?

## How does this truth apply to me?

- What is my relationship to the human author?
- What is my relationship to the orginal audience?
- What are the differences between my world and their world?
- Is my relationship to God different than theirs?
